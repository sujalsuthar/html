Energy Conservation Ideas

Ordered List

  1. Turn off lights and electronics when not in use.
  2. Use energy-efficient LED light bulbs.
  3. Unplug chargers and appliances when they're not needed.
  4. Weatherproof your home to prevent drafts and save heating/cooling energy.
  5. Carpool, bike, walk, or use public transportation to reduce fuel consumption.

Unordered List

  • Plant trees and shrubs to provide shade and reduce cooling energy needs.
  • Seal gaps and cracks in windows and doors to prevent energy leakage.
  • Use a programmable thermostat to optimize heating and cooling schedules.
  • Turn off water when brushing teeth and fix leaks to conserve water heating energy.
  • Opt for energy-efficient appliances with high ENERGY STAR ratings.
